Steps to Generate a Google App Password

  1. Go to your Google Account Security Settings.
  2. Under "Signing in to Google", enable 2-Step Verification.
  3. After that, click on App Passwords and re-enter your password if asked.
  4. Select "Mail" as the app and "Other (Custom name)" as the device.
  5. Enter a name like SCM Mail App and click Generate.
  6. Copy the generated 16-character password.
  7. Paste it into the field and click Save.
